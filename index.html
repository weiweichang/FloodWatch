About

FloodWatch MY uses live data from MET Malaysia (weather warnings) and Public Infobanjir/JPS (river gauge stations). Data is refreshed every 5 minutes. Elevation data from Open-Elevation API. This tool is for informational purposes only.

What does "Watch" mean?
MET Malaysia has issued a weather warning for this area — such as heavy rain or thunderstorms. Monitor conditions and prepare. No immediate danger yet.
🟠
What does "Flood Alert" mean?
A flood-specific warning is active. Check water levels at Public Infobanjir. Know your nearest evacuation centre (PPS) and be ready to move.
🔴
What does "High Urgency" mean?
Immediate risk. Call 999 if in danger. Move to higher ground. Do not drive through flooded roads. Follow NADMA official guidance.
